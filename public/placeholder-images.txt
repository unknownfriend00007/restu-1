# Placeholder Images for Restaurant Website

Replace these placeholder images with your actual restaurant photos:

## Hero Image
- Path: /hero.jpg
- Recommended size: 1920x1080px
- Should show your restaurant interior or signature dish

## Logo
- Path: /logo.png
- Recommended size: 200x200px (square)
- Transparent background preferred

## Dish Images
- Path: /dish1.jpg, /dish2.jpg, /dish3.jpg
- Recommended size: 400x300px
- High-quality photos of your signature dishes

## Additional Images
You can add more images in the /public/images/ folder:
- Restaurant exterior
- Interior ambiance
- Chef at work
- Happy customers

## Image Optimization Tips
- Use WebP format for better compression
- Compress images to under 500KB each
- Use descriptive alt text for SEO
- Ensure mobile-friendly aspect ratios
